Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is my go-to brown butter chocolate chip cookie recipe that I found in high school.

Ingredients

  • salt
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • broccoli
  • 1/2 bottle red curry sauce from TJ's
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice

Steps

  1. Mince garlic and ginger, slice chicken
  2. Cook chicken with some oil, ginger, and salt. Remove from heat.
  3. Cook onions with pinch of salt
  4. Add other veggies and cook for a few minutes
  5. Add coconut milk, curry sauce, and water if needed
  6. Simmer for a few minutes until veggies mostly cooked
  7. Add chicken and simmer to combine
  8. Turn heat on low, add soy sauce and lime juice.
  9. Serve with white rice!

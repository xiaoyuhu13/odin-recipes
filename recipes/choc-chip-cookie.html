Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is my go-to brown butter chocolate chip cookie recipe that I found in high school. (bakingthegoods.com)

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar (unpacked)
  • 1/2 cup white sugar (little less)
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder (take out to reduce fluffiness)
  • chocolate chips (dark, milk, semisweet or combo!)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Combine dry ingredients in a bowl
  3. Brown 1 stick of butter
  4. Cream other stick of butter with sugar and vanilla
  5. Add semi-cooled brown butter to wet ingredients stirring constantly
  6. Add eggs 1 by 1 stirring to combine after each one
  7. Add dry ingredients in 2 batches to prevent over mixing
  8. Add chocolate chips!
  9. Bake cookies for 10-12 minutes
  10. Add more chocolate chips on top right after taking out of oven for ~aesthetics~
  11. Eat warm cookies with milk :drool
